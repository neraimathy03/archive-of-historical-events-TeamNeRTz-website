ANCIENT PERIOD


The ancient period, also known as antiquity, refers to the time in human history from the beginning of recorded history around 3000 BCE to the fall of the Western Roman Empire in 476 CE. This period includes the development and flourishing of early civilizations such as Mesopotamia, Ancient Egypt, Ancient Greece, and Ancient Rome. Key advancements in writing, governance, architecture, and science were made during this era, laying the foundations for subsequent historical developments.
